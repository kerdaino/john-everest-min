About The President

Apostle Everest Okoli

Man of Power, Fire, and Prayer

Okoli Everestus Chidolue John is a Revivalist, a Discipler, a Teacher par excellence, an Apostle to Nations, and a Prophet of God. Born on January 26, 1991, in Anambra State, Nigeria, he gave his life to Christ in 2007.

An erudite Criminologist and theologian, he founded John Everest Ministries Int'l in 2020. He has been influenced by the ministry of Apostle Arome Osayi. He is the founder of African Youths Initiative Programs (AYIP) and the convener of the International World Revival Conference.
